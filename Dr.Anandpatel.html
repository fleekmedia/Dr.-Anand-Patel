Profile :

  • Dr. Anand Patel is a Consultant Gynaecologist, ART clinician, and Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon with over 20 years of experience and a Diploma in Advanced Gynec Endoscopy from CICE, France, as well as a Diploma in Sonography.   • Currently, he serves as the Director at Motherhood Women's & Child Care Hospital and Midas Touch Cosmetic Centre. At Motherhood, they have established the first IVF hospital in Sola, Ahmedabad, with a vision to offer qualitative services through continuous research and market study. Their team comprises over 20 highly skilled specialists in IVF, gynecology, high-risk pregnancy, genetics, Gynec Oncology, endoscopy Full 4K 3D Laparoscopy and 3D/4D sonography.
  • Their achievements include treating over 1Lac satisfied patients, with more than 14,000 successful IVF cases. They have also established 35+ OPD sub-centers across Gujarat to extend their reach. In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Patel actively supports women's and children's health awareness programs and conducts medical camps in underprivileged areas.
  • The hospital is NABH Certified and dedicated to delivering specialized care for women and children, focusing on IVF, Gynec endoscopic procedures, high-risk pregnancies, and NICU/PICU services. Their mission is to provide exceptional care with the highest professional quality.
